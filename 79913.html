Update 1: Season 1 Report
Halo Championship Series

Season 1 of The Halo Championship Series is underway and it's not over yet. With two crucial tournaments to go before the $100,000 Finals at PAX East, the HCS Update will give you all the details on what's happened so far and prep you for the Gamers For Giving and Battle of Europe tournaments happening the weekend of February 21. Plus, we spotlight two of competitive Halo's finest players: Snip3down from Evil Geniuses and Chig from Denial eSports.

Ep 5: Training Daze
Red vs. Blue - Season 12

Training begins for the Reds, Blues, and their chosen recruits as they try to prove they are ready for the upcoming rescue mission. The training relies around capturing and disarming the reluctant but formidable Felix, and unfortunately early attempts are only successful at turning into a comedy of errors. If that weren’t bad enough, the embarrassing performance is captured by the squad’s helmet cams and put on display at the New Republic HQ.

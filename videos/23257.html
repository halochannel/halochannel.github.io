Halo Announcement
E3 2014 Media Briefing

For the first time ever, The Master Chief's entire story is on one console with Halo: The Master Chief Collection. Featuring a re-mastered Halo 2: Anniversary, along with Halo: Combat Evolved Anniversary, Halo 3, and Halo 4, as well as the new digital series, Halo: Nightfall, and access to the Halo 5: Guardians Beta, this is the definitive Halo experience.

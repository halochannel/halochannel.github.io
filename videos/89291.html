Iron Games Atlanta - HCS

Iron Games Atlanta was the first Halo Championship Series LAN tournament of Season 2. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for $25,000 in prizing and over 2000 HCS Points. The tournament ran from April 17th to April 19th at Georgia World Congress Center in Atlanta, Georgia.

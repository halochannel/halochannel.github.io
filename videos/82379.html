HCS Season 1 Finals

The Halo Championship Series Season 1 Finals at PAX East was the final LAN tournament of the season and determined who would become the HCS Season 1 Champions. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for a $100,000 prize purse with $50,000 going to the winning team. The tournament ran from March 6th to March 8th at PAX East in Boston.

Gamers For Giving

Gamers For Giving was the third Halo Championship Series LAN Event. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for over $10,000 and HCS points. The event ran on February 21st and 22nd at Eastern Michigan University in Ypsilanti, Michigan.

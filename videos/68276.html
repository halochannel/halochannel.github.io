Championship Match - Series 1
UGC St. Louis

UGC St. Louis was the second Halo Championship Series LAN Event. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for over $20,000 and HCS points. The event ran from January 2nd - 4th at the Airport Hilton Hotel in St. Louis, MO. This is the first of two series.

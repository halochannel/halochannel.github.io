Halo World Championship
$1,000,000 Prize Pool

The Halo 5: Guardians Arena multiplayer experience was built for high-stakes competitive play with a focus on eSports. To kick off the Halo 5: Guardians eSports story, 343 Industries, along with ESL, MLG, Twitch, YouTube Gaming are announcing the Halo World Championship - with a prize pool starting at $1,000,000.

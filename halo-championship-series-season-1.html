Season 1 Finals

The Halo Championship Series Season 1 Finals at PAX East was the final LAN tournament of the season and determined who would become the HCS Season 1 Champions. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for a $100,000 prize purse with $50,000 going to the winning team.

Gamers for Giving

Gamers For Giving was the third Halo Championship Series LAN Event. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for over $10,000 and HCS points. The event ran on February 21st and 22nd at Eastern Michigan University in Ypsilanti, Michigan.

EGL Battle of Europe

The Battle of Europe, presented by EGL, was the European wildcard LAN tournament for Season 1 of the Halo Championship Series. Players from across Europe competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for €10,000, a trip to the HCS Season 1 Finals at PAX East in Boston, and a coveted wildcard spot at the Finals.

UGC St. Louis

UGC St. Louis was the second Halo Championship Series LAN Event. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for over $20,000 and HCS points. The event ran from January 2 - 4 in St. Louis, Missouri.

Iron Games Columbus

Iron Games Columbus was the first Halo Championship Series LAN event. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for over $15,000 and HCS points. The event ran from December 12 - 14 in Columbus, Ohio.

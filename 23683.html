Ep 3: Prophets
The Sprint - Season 1

With the map "Truth" well on its way to completion, the art team must shift their focus to the remix map "Regret," a re-imagined version of "Truth" that needs to maintain the essence of the original map, but also offer players a new way to play. While this is a massive undertaking in and of itself, the question remains: is there enough time to finish it before the deadline?

World Premiere Exhibition Match
Halo 5: Guardians

From HaloFest 2014 in Los Angeles, 343 Industries brings you the world premiere of the Halo 5: Guardians Multiplayer Beta. Halo pro teams, Str8 Rippin and Optic Gaming, test new Spartan Abilities in a no-holds-barred Slayer match on Truth - a reimagining of the Halo 2 fan-favorite Midship. The Halo 5: Guardians Multiplayer Beta is available on December 29th only in the Master Chief Collection.

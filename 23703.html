Assault on Squad 45 is a series of toymation shorts developed by MEGA Brands, the producers of the Halo Wars Mega Bloks sets. In part 4, Plans go awry when the Covenant launches a full scale counter-attack to rescue the Arbiter and the UNSC struggles to repel the offensive while keeping the Arbiter imprisoned.

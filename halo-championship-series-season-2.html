Season 2 Finals

Season 2 Finals was the third Halo Championship Series LAN tournament of Season 2. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for $150,000 in prizing. The tournament ran from July 24th to July 26th at the ESL Studios in Burbank, California.

PGL Indianapolis

PGL Indianapolis was the second Halo Championship Series LAN tournament of Season 2. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for $50,000 in prizing. The tournament ran from June 26th to June 28th at the Indiana Convention Center in Indianapolis, Indiana.

Iron Games Atlanta

Iron Games Atlanta was the first Halo Championship Series LAN tournament of Season 2. Players competed in Halo 2: Anniversary for $25,000 in prizing and over 2000 HCS Points.
